My League of Legends Journey A 3-Month Reality Check

Special thanks to my friend who suffered through 5 matches just to lift my ban!

After actually playing League for a while (thanks to a persistent friend who literally played 5 matches to clear my suspension), I've come to realize that my initial impressions were... let's say "incomplete." Here's what I've learned after being forced back into the Rift:

The "Sudden" You Have Been Slain!

It's not sudden at all. It's about spacing, farming, and item differences. When a higher-level champion enters your range, you're basically already dead if you're not prepared. It's not sudden—it's a calculated defeat.

It is OKAY to play against Bots

You don't have to prove yourself by jumping into intermediate mode. Playing against intro AI bots helps you master skills, movement, and game mechanics without the pressure. Take your time learning before facing real players.

Strategic & Calm Thinking Required

It's about choosing your battles wisely—knowing when to push and when to retreat. This game rewards calm, calculated players who think before acting, not those who rush in blindly.

Friends Change Everything

The harmony and teamwork with friends make the game significantly more enjoyable. They encourage you to stay even when you're not having fun alone, turning frustrating moments into memorable ones.

Understanding The Ban Policy

Going AFK ruins your teammates' experience. In bot lane, it becomes 1v2 (plus minions!), which is basically a guaranteed loss. While I still find it harsh, I understand why the penalties exist. (Though I'm still salty about it!)

The Clicking Marathon

The endless clicking is exhausting! But I've heard rumors Riot is considering keyboard movement options. Maybe my prayers (and previous website) were heard after all?

The Final Realization

League is actually a good game—it just doesn't suit my personality.

As someone who pushes aggressively and isn't always calm, and who sometimes wants to relax without intense strategic thinking, it's not the right fit for me.

My mood swings from "I wanna play!" to "J'ai plus envie" too quickly for a game that demands consistent focus.

So yes, I still have issues with League... but now I understand it better. Maybe it's not the game, but the player? (Still won't admit that publicly though!)
